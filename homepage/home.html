IMMORTALS
About Us

Team Immortals are athletes who represent Thiagarajar College of Engineering, Madurai in major athletic events and have won medals in various competitions. They also compete for the college in the Anna University Zone 16. In addition, they organize events within the college campus with the help of THIDALS, a sports organizing and development committee at TCE. Some athletes from the team also contribute to THIDALS in organizing other sports events.
